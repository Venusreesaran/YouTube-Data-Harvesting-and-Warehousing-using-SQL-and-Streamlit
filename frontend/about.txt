About This App

This is a YouTube Analytics Dashboard built using Streamlit and Flask.
It allows users to analyze YouTube channel statistics, such as the top 
most-viewed videos, most liked videos, and channel analytics, among others.

Features:
1. View the top 10 most viewed and liked videos.
2. See the total views for each channel.
3. Analyze comments, likes, and dislikes across videos.
4. View a yearly breakdown of published videos for channels.
